Let's cook a delicious Lasagna!

Just like how grandmother used to make

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  4. Add minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with half of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by one-third of the meat sauce.
  8. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
