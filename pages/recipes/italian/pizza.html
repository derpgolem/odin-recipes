Let's cook a delicious Pizza!

It's Pizza time!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Brush the dough lightly with olive oil to prevent sogginess.
  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  7. Add the sliced pepperoni, black olives, and green bell pepper on top of the cheese.
  8. Sprinkle dried oregano and basil over the toppings for added flavor.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
